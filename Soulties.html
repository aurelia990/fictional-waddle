Karma & Rebirth – The Eternal Cycle of the Soul

Introduction

What happens after we die? This ancient question finds answers in karma, samsara, and the soul's journey through rebirths.

Karma: Cause and Effect

Karma is energy. Good deeds bring blessings. Bad deeds return as pain. Every action matters and echoes through lifetimes.

Karma: The Law of Cause and Effect

The Soul (Atman)

The soul is eternal, evolving through births. Each life is a chance to grow closer to the Divine.

Heaven and Hell

These are temporary stops based on karma—not permanent destinations. The soul eventually returns to Earth for further lessons.

Reincarnation

The soul can take human, animal, or celestial forms depending on karma. The cycle continues until moksha is attained.

Past Life Ties

We meet people from past lives—some we loved, some we hurt. Be kind. Life is a mirror. Perhaps they are here to help you heal or repay a bond once shared.

Instant connections, dreams, or fears may be echoes from a past life. Those who challenge you could be your greatest spiritual allies in disguise.

Amazing thought: Perhaps the ones who hurt or test us most are the ones who loved us the most in another lifetime—and chose to help us grow through hardship in this one.

Moksha: Liberation

Freedom from the cycle. Total peace. Pure awareness. The soul's final goal.

Live Mindfully

Act with love and awareness. Each soul you meet may be linked to you by a golden thread of time.

So next time you meet someone, pause and wonder: What thread of eternity might connect our hearts?
