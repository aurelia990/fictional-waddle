Karma: The Law of Cause and Effect

Karma is not fate. It is the energy created by our actions, thoughts, and intentions. Every deed, no matter how small, carries consequences.

  • Good karma leads to positive outcomes and spiritual growth.
  • Bad karma results in suffering or spiritual setbacks.

This accumulated karma determines not only our experiences in this life but also our circumstances in future lives.

The Soul (Atman): Eternal and Evolving

The soul, or atman, is eternal. It transcends physical death and carries the blueprint of our consciousness across lifetimes. Each birth provides an opportunity for the soul to learn, evolve, and ultimately reach liberation.

Heaven and Hell: Temporary Realms

Unlike the Western concept of eternal heaven or hell, Eastern philosophies view them as transitory states:

  • Heaven (Swarga): A temporary reward for good karma.
  • Hell (Naraka): A temporary punishment for bad karma.

The soul eventually returns to the earthly plane to continue its journey.
